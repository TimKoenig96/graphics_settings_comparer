The benchmarks are to be taken with a grain of salt, as they may be inaccurate due to drivers, patches, different component architecture and lots of other factors.

To save bandwidth, I am resizing all images using RIOT. I set the format to JPEG, 95% quality, without any chroma subsampling. That should be better than what you need, and hopefully not cause a huge amount of bandwidth usage.
